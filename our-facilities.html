The Manchester Makerspace is located at 36 Old Granite Street In Manchester New Hampshire USA.

We occupy approximately 6500 square feet of the building on the first floor and second floor, with room to expand on the second floor in the future.

#Parking

Member parking is located on the east side of our building near the woodshop dock.  Members shall have a parking sticker in the front passenger-side windshield of the car (provided at orientation) if you have a second vehicle without a sticker please mention this in slack in the #Parking channel so you are not towed.  Parking in front of the building on Old Granite Street is metered public parking. 

Overflow parking is located behind the makerspace as indicated on the map below. Members will be expected  to have a makerspace parking sticker for the overflow lot as well.   Warning:  The overflow lot is often paid parking during major events, this is out of our control.

Additionally, there are metered spaces on portions of Elm, and (free on weekends and holidays) Old Granite and Depot.

Members shall only park in either lot while actively present and using the space, (with leeway for meals and parts runs).     Vehicles abandoned in either parking area will be rendered down for parts and fuel.
