# Things The Space Always Needs
- Utility knife blades
- Paintbrushes
- Painter's / masking tape... especially super wide tape.
- Wood Glue (Titebond III preferred)
- Acetone, odorless mineral spirits
- Hacksaw blades
- Abrasives
  - Sandpaper (flat sheets and orbital pads)
  - Scotchbrite
- High quality driver bits (we have plenty of trash ones)
- High quality drillbits (see below)
- Protective equipment
  - Safety glasses
  - Earbuds
  - Nitrile gloves
- Printer filament
  - 1.75mm diameter
  - PLA
  - ABS
- Tape measures
- Machinist's rulers
- Cleaning supplies
  
We also have an [Amazon Wishlist](https://www.amazon.com/hz/wishlist/genericItemsPage/RB06Y5US8KJL?ref_=wl_share) but it isn't always up-to-date, and not everything is on Amazon (or worth buying from there).
