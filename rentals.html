Available storage rentals

  • “Totes” in racks (monthly invoice, no limit on duration) $15/month
  • Shelves (monthly invoice, 3 month cap) Starts at $20/month for ½ shelf
  • Floor plots (monthly invoice, 3 month cap) Contact the board
  • Auto Bay (for active projects going beyond 8 hours out of 24, or spanning multiple days)  $25/day, by exception only, with resource manager pre-approval
  • Parking (for emergencies only) $25/day, only with board pre-approval.   Vehicles left longer than a day without written board approval will be removed at vehicle owner's expense.
     

MMS is a shared workshop, with a formal carry in, carry out policy (unless you rent a storage space).   

If you are not present and actively working on your project, all your property must be removed or located within the bounds of your rented storage.  Neither members nor their guests are permitted to “store” (abandon beyond their line of sight) personal property anywhere other than in a storage space rented to them.    The sole exception to this policy is storage of safety gear in the locker room.

Makerspace property (tools, etc) need to be returned to the proper storage location so they can be used by other members. 

We understand sometimes items freshly painted, stained or glued up need to dry or welded may need to cool but in cases like this you should use the least space possible cleaning up the rest of the mess, and leave a note about who the project belongs to and when you will return.  Please include contact information on this note so if there is an issue or it needs to be moved you can be contacted.
