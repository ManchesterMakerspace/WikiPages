Manual Management

The spreadsheet tracking current volunteer credits can be found here.  Do NOT move or copy the spreadsheet to a different location. Automated software uses its location to automatically update it.

Adding a credit event

  1. On the Volunteer Spreadsheet sheet, add the date of the event in Row 1
  2. On the Date Details sheet, include a short description of what the volunteer credit is for
  3. Back on the Volunteer Spreadsheet, under the column of the date that you added in Step 1 above, enter your initials for each member that participated in the volunteer event. NOTE: The spreadsheet may need to be unfiltered to see all members.
  4. Thats it. Do not update the required or completed count. Do not include anything for members that did not participate. Automated software described below will update those columns. 

Automated Management

On the last day of every month, automated software will parse this spreadsheet and complete the following actions:

  • Add missing members
  • Update required credits for the year per member

Monthly Updates

At the end of every month, software checks membership snapshots (lists of who were members on a particular day) and determine what members were active for more than 21 days that month. If they were active, their requirement count for the year increases based on the schedule listed here.

Additionally, software will go through any volunteer dates and look for blank cells for members. If they were a member at that day, it will mark the cell with a dash, and if they weren't a member, then it marks "N/A". These notations not really matter for the total count but act as a sanity check for review.

The completed column counts how many volunteer days have someone's initials in them. (It will count anything thats not empty, or - or N/A)
