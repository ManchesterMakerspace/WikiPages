Join the Makerspace

 

From our website:

  1. Click one of the payment buttons found on our website at manchestermakerspace.org/membership
  2. Proceed through the PayPal payment process
  3. Upon completion, the Makerspace will receive a notification of the payment and contact you to sign up in the digital makerspace software at members.manchestermakerspace.org. You can register there on your own before hearing from the Makerspace though.

 

Digital Makerspace Signup:

  1. Go to members.manchestermakerspace.org
  2. Click the “Register” button on the home page to start the registration process
  3. Fill our your details in the “Create an Account” form and submit
  4. Once accepted, you’ll be redirected to your Profile and presented with a Welcome screen
  5. Select the blue “Review Documents” button to complete the Code of Conduct and Member Contract agreements
  6. Accept the two agreements by marking the checkboxes and signing where appropriate
  7. After completion, the Makerspace will receive another notification and contact you directly about getting a key fob and giving you an orientation.
  8. At this point, you have registered but your membership has not actually started. Your membership will start once you receive your key fob.
