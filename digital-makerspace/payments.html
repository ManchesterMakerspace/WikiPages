Making a Payment

Payments for subscriptions will occur automatically once you complete the initial payment. Manual payments are only necessary for one time charges or the initial payments just referenced.

 

Paying dues:

  1. Any dues that need to be paid manually will appear on your profile page and display the due date. If it says “Automatic payment on <date>”, then you can expect that due to be paid automatically with your default payment method on the specified date.
  2. Login and view your member profile
  3. Click the “Dues” tab within your profile.
  4. Select the checkbox for dues that you would like to pay at this time and click the blue “Pay membership dues” button. Note: If Due Date is ““Automatic payment on <date>”, it cannot be paid manually.
  5. Upon selecting “Pay membership dues”, you’ll be taken to the Checkout screen. Instructions for the Checkout screen may be found below.

Checkout:

  1. When reaching the checkout screen, the dues you’re about to pay will appear on the right hand side and the payment method options will appear on the left hand side.
  2. Add a payment option if one does not already exist. NOTE: If creating a subscription, the payment option used will be the default payment method for future payments unless changed later. Instructions to change the default payment method can be found here.
  3. Select your desired payment option from the list.
  4. Review the dues that are about to be paid and click “Submit Payment”
  5. Once the payment completes (may take some time to finish loading), you will be redirected to a receipt screen where you can review what you’ve paid and the affects of those dues. Please print this screen for your records
  6. Completed payments may be reviewed at a later date from your profile in the “Payment History” section.

Manage Payment Methods

  1. These instructions are for adding and removing payment methods. You may find the instructions for changing your default payment method here.
  2. Login and view your member profile
  3. Select Account Settings and then Payment Methods from the left menu within settings.

Adding a Payment Method

  1. Click blue “Add New Payment Method” button to open the payment method form
  2. Select a payment method type: Credit Card or PayPal.
  3. If Credit Card selected:
  4. Enter your card number, expiration, CSV and zip code and submit.
  5. If accepted, the form will close and your new payment option will be displayed in the list of payment options
  6. If PayPal selected:
  7. PayPal will open another screen for you to login and enter your payment information.
  8. If accepted, PayPal will redirect you back to our site where your PayPal payment option will be available

Deleting a Payment Method

  1. Click the red and white “Delete Payment Method” button and accept the prompt.
