Payment History and Refunds

All payments are non-refundable, but you may request the administrators to review a transaction for a refund if you believe the transaction to be a mistake. Only payments made through this software can be refunded in this manner. Payments made through PayPal or Eventbrite must be requested separately by emailing us at contact@manchestermakerspace.org.

 

Review Payment History

  1. Login and view your member profile
  2. Navigate to the “Payment Methods” tab. If this tab is not visible, then you do not have transactions made through this software that can be requested for refunds

Requesting a refund

  1. Login and view your member profile
  2. Navigate to the “Payment Methods” tab. If this tab is not visible, then you do not have transactions made through this software that can be requested for refunds
  3. Select the checkbox for the transaction you wish to be refunded and click “Request Refund” button. Note: Only transactions marked as Paid may be refunded. Transactions still processing cannot be refunded until they have cleared.
  4. Submit the dialog that appears after you click the “Request Refund” button.
  5. Administrators will receive a notification about this refund and review it within 7 days.
  6. If the refund is accepted, you will receive a notification of the refund being processed.
