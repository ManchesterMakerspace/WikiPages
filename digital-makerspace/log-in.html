Log In

Member accounts are created during sign up. If you did not create an account yourself, one was automatically created for you. However, if it was created for you, it will not have a password associated so you’ll need to use the Password Reset instructions below before you’re able to login.

 

Logging In

Go to members.manchestermakerspace.org/

Click “Already a member? Login” button in upper right hand corner

Enter your credentials and click submit.

If successful, you’ll be redirected to your profile page

Password Reset

If you forgot your password or never set one when you signed up, these instructions can help you reset it. If you run into any issues during this process, please contact an admin.

Go to members.manchestermakerspace.org/

Click “Already a member? Login” button in upper right hand corner

Click the “Forgot your password” link

Enter the email address associated with your account and submit. Note, for security reasons, we do not validate that the email address was correct. If an account with that email address exists in our system, a reset email will be delivered

Check the email account of the address you entered for the Password Reset email. Within that email, there is a link to complete the reset process. This link is valid 6 hours after requesting your password to be reset. If you do not use that link in that time frame, you will need to start over.

After clicking the link, enter a new password in the displayed form and click submit. If successful, you’ll be automatically logged in to your account.
