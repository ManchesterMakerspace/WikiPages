Manage Membership

 How to add, modify, end a Manchester Makerspace membership subscription via https;//members.manchestermakerspace.org/

 

Create new membership subscription

  1. Login and view your member profile
  2. Select Account Settings and then Membership from the left menu within settings.
  3. Your current membership status is displayed, including the type. Note: Membership Type must be ‘Month-to-month’ or ‘No membership found’ in order to create new membership subscription through this process. If it says 'Managed by PayPal', please follow the "Cancel PayPal Subscription" instructions below before proceeding.
  4. Select the “Update Membership” button to view a list of membership options
  5. Upon selecting a membership option, you’ll be taken to the Checkout screen. Instructions for the Checkout process may be found here.

Pause \ Suspend memberships 

Memberships can not be “paused”.  If you wish to put your membership on hold and stop the charges to your payment method you must cancel your subscription prior to the next renewal period by following the Cancel membership instructions below. 

Save your key fob! When you reactivate your subscription your old key fob will activate immediately.  To un-pause or reactivate your membership follow the Create membership subscription instructions above.  Upon successfully creating a membership subscription your payment method will be charged and your key fob will be reactivated  immediately,  

If you need a new key fob use the #discuss-talk-to-the-board channel in slack, or stop in during Open House hours.

Cancel membership subscription 

  1. Login and view your member profile
  2. Select Account Settings and then Membership from the left menu within settings.
  3. Your current membership status is displayed, including the type. Note: Membership Type must be Subscription in order to cancel your membership through this process. If it says ‘Managed by PayPal’, please follow the "Cancel PayPal Subscription" instructions below before proceeding.
  4. Select the “Cancel Membership” button and accept the dialog to cancel your membership.
  5. Your key will remain active until your expiration date, which can be found on your profile page.

Changing membership type

  1. You may change your membership type by cancelling your current membership and selecting a new one following the above instructions.
  2. Your key will remain active until your expiration date, which can be found on your profile page.
  3. Upon starting the new membership type, your payment method will be charged immediately and the additional subscription period will be added to your existing expiration date. This means you will receive full credit for any remaining time on your prior subscription.

Changing payment method

  1. Login and view your member profile.  Note: If your membership type says ‘Managed by PayPal’, you must login to paypal.com to cancel your subscription payment before you can change your payment method on our site.
  2. Select Account Settings and then Membership from the left menu within settings.
  3. Click the blue “Change Payment Method” button to open the form
  4. Select a different payment method or add a new one, and then submit the form.
  5. The selected payment option will be used during the following payment cycle.

Cancel a PayPal-managed Subscription

  1. Login to paypal.com and navigate to "Settings" by using the gear icon in the upper right corner and select the "Payments" tab. Or you may navigate directly to https://www.paypal.com/myaccount/settings/payments 
  2. Select the Manchester Makerspace membership.
  3. Click the cancel button and confirm the cancellation.
  4. Return to above procedure if applicable
