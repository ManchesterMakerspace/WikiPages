Components

Metal stock and components (wheels, motors, hinges, handles, etc.) that members can use for projects are all along the south wall. Take or leave as you need, but try to keep like things together.

Special Rules

  • Keep the paint booth closed to reduce dust in the room.
  • Cover all paint overspray in or out of the room.
  • The work tables don't need to be left perfect, but they need to be flat. Clean up all paint/oil/debris.

Room for Improvement
