What's the best tool to use?

The question is almost always more complicated, but here's a rough guide:

“I want to cut fancy shapes out of flat metal, but I don't care about edge surface finish that much” → Plasma Cutter

“I want to cut simple shapes out of flat metal” → Metal Shear, Vertical Bandsaw (depends on material)

“I want to cut stock to length.” → Horizontal bandsaw or cold saw

“I want to make precise, mostly square things” → Mill

“I want to make fancy, precise things" → CNC Mill

“I want to make round things” → Lathe

“I want to make fancy round things” → CNC Lathe

“I want to join two pieces of metal. I don't have much welding experience.” → MIG Welder

“I want to join two pieces of metal. I want it to look good and be very strong.” → TIG Welder

“Can't be stuck if it's liquid” → Oxyacetylene Torch
