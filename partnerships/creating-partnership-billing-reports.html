Partners are billed for the discounted portion of their employee memberships. To generate a report for this billing from the UI, complete the following steps

  1. Navigate to https://makerspace-test.herokuapp.com/billing/transactions 
  2. Click the “Filters” button
  3. In the Filters, select the date range of the report
  4. In the Filters, elect the discounts for the company that you are building the report for
  5. Click “Download Table” button
  6. The resulting CSV will contain a list of discounts and the amounts. Each row is a unique transaction. The company is responsible for the sum of all discounts listed in the table.
